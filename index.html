What is the Status icon?
expand_more
It shows if a student moved up (thumb_up), down (thumb_down), or stayed the same (-) based on class rankings from 2025 to 2026.
person_search
How do I search for a student?
expand_more
You can use the search bar to type their English Name, Chinese Name, or Student ID.
star
How do I save a student?
expand_more
Tap any student and click the Star icon (star) at the top right, or Long Press the student's name on the list.
view_agenda
What is Compact View?
expand_more
Compact view shrinks the student list and hides avatars so you can see more students at once on your screen.
history
How do I clear my search history?
expand_more
Tap the search bar and click the trash can icon next to your recent searches.
